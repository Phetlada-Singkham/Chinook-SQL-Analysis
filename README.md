# Chinook SQL Analysis

## Objective
Analyze customer purchasing behavior to identify high-value customers and business opportunities.

## Tools
- SQLite

## Skills Used
- JOIN
- GROUP BY
- Aggregation
- DISTINCT
- ORDER BY
- LIMIT

## Key Analysis
1. Total spending by each customer
2. Distribution of customers across countries
3. Identification of top 5 high-value customers

## Key Insights
- A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total revenue
- Customer base is distributed across multiple countries, indicating global reach
- High-value customers can be identified for targeted marketing or retention strategies

## Recommendations
- Focus on retaining top customers through loyalty programs
- Develop targeted campaigns for high-spending customers
- Explore expansion opportunities in countries with strong customer presence

## Dataset
Chinook sample database
